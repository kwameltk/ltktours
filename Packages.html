Experience Kwahu Traditional Area

Whatsapp / Weekend Escape / Hotel Pickups

You may not have heard of the wonders of kwahu aside the most talked about Kwahu Easter Festival with main attractions being Paragliding, commercial marketing, Brand Visibility, Family Home coming and Carnival Entertainment. If you’ve ever thought of a getaway to a place where you can experience the finest of nature attractions that can take your breath? Then kwahu is that place.

You can experience a heavy flow of waterfalls at a 60ft umbrella rock in Tafo, Cruise in a Canoe on the Afram Lake, Hike the tallest Bruku Sacred Rock, make wishes in pitch darkness ancestral cave, get lost at the Amartey Stonecity and fly on the Odweanoma Mountain.

THE KWAHU MOUNTAINS ARE EASILY ACCESSIBLE FROM ACCRA AND KUMASI

Nkawkaw is located approximately between Accra and Kumasi with 2hrs 40min maximum travel hours from both cities. Pick up and drop off services available by LTK LYFT Services. The central location and the fact that Nkawkaw is a transport hub, makes the Kwahu Mountains very suitable for a 3-day 2 Night trip. Thinking of traveling through public transport? You can easily go to the public transport in your area and ask of Nkawkaw Bus,Enjoy your journey to kwahu.
