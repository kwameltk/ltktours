Team Profile

Our CEO, Kwame Owusu Bright ,a visionary explorer with a profound commitment to showcasing Kwahu's treasures, leads 'Let's Tour Kwahu' with unwavering passion and a dedication to responsible travel.

Our dedicated team at LTK Tours popularly known as 'Let's Tour Kwahu' comprises passionate travel enthusiasts, experienced guides, and cultural ambassadors deeply connected to the heart of Kwahu. With a diverse range of expertise, our team members bring a wealth of knowledge about the region's history, traditions, and natural wonders. Their commitment to delivering exceptional experiences and their genuine love for sharing the beauty and culture of Kwahu ensure that each journey with us is both memorable and transformative.
