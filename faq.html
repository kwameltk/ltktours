FAQ'S

Where is Kwahu Located ?

Kwahu is conveniently located between Accra and Kumasi, with a maximum travel time of 3 hours. For tourists and travelers heading to Kwahu, the journey typically begins with finding a nearby station offering transportation to Nkawkaw. Upon arrival in Nkawkaw, taxis are readily available to take you to your desired destination. For a hassle-free experience and reliable pick-up service, contact us directly.

What types of tours do LTK Tours offer?

LTK Tours offers a variety of experiences including adventure tours, cultural experiences, hiking excursions, and weekend escape packages. We cater to group and private tour requests to ensure a personalized experience for all our clients.

How can I book a tour with LTK Tours?

Booking a tour is simple! You can book directly through our website, call our customer service line, or visit our office in Kwahu. Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive a detailed itinerary and confirmation email. BOOK A TOUR

What is included in the tour package?

Our tour PACKAGES typically include accommodation, meals, guided tours, entrance fees to attractions, and transportation. Specific inclusions may vary depending on the package chosen, so please refer to the tour details on our website or contact us for more information.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made more than 14 days before the tour start date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 2 days of the tour start date may incur a cancellation fee of 5% of the total package fee. Please check our full cancellation policy on our website for specific details.

How do I make a payment for my tour?

Payments can be made securely online through our website using major credit card bank transfers or mobile money PAY HERE. We also accept payments in our office if you prefer to pay in person. Once the payment is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Are there any discounts available for group bookings?

Yes, we offer 10% special discounts for group bookings above 20 pax. Please get in touch with our customer service team with the details of your group, and we will provide you with a customized quote and applicable discounts.

What should I pack for my tour?

We recommend packing comfortable clothing and footwear suitable for outdoor activities, especially hiking. Depending on the season and specific activities, you might also need sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, a rain jacket, and a camera for the views. Detailed information will be provided once your booking is confirmed.

Is transportation provided during the tour?

Yes, our tours include transportation. We provide comfortable and reliable transport services from the pickup point in Nkawkaw to all the tour destinations. We can also assist you with organizing a pickup for you and your friends from your departure which is mostly a special arrangement. Our vehicles are well-maintained to ensure a smooth journey.

Can I customize my tour itinerary?

Absolutely! We offer customizable tour options to cater to your specific interests and preferences. Please contact us to discuss your requirements, and we will work with you to create a tailored itinerary that meets your needs and your budget too.
